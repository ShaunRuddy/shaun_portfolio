About

Engineer by Training,
Builder by Nature

I hold a Master of Engineering in Robotics & AI from the University of Toronto, alongside dual Bachelor's degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Computing Technology from the University of Ottawa.

Currently working as a Robotics Perception Engineer in Ossining, NY, I specialize in deploying multi-sensor software pipelines on embedded platforms like the NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano, developing infrared processing algorithms, and pursuing SLAM and reinforcement learning research.

I've also developed a novel non-destructive testing system capable of detecting micron-scale fatigue cracks — work that has attracted interest from the U.S. Air Force.

Outside the lab, I've captained a competitive ultimate frisbee team to a provincial championship and was a provincial robotics tournament finalist.
